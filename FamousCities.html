Famous Cities

Explore the world's most iconic destinations.

Paris, France

Top Attractions: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral

Local Food: Croissants, macarons, escargot

Best Time to Visit: Apr - Jun, Sep - Oct

Interesting Fact: The Eiffel Tower was originally meant to be temporary!

Tokyo, Japan

Top Attractions: Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, Meiji Shrine

Local Food: Sushi, ramen, tempura

Best Time to Visit: Mar - May, Oct - Nov

Interesting Fact: Tokyo is the largest metropolitan area in the world!

New York City, USA

Top Attractions: Statue of Liberty, Times Square, Central Park

Local Food: New York-style pizza, bagels, cheesecake

Best Time to Visit: Apr - Jun, Sep - Nov

Interesting Fact: Over 800 languages are spoken in NYC!

Rome, Italy

Top Attractions: Colosseum, Vatican City, Trevi Fountain

Local Food: Pasta carbonara, gelato, pizza

Best Time to Visit: Apr - Jun, Sep - Oct

Interesting Fact: Ancient Romans invented concrete!

Sydney, Australia

Top Attractions: Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge, Bondi Beach

Local Food: Meat pies, lamingtons, barramundi

Best Time to Visit: Sep - Nov, Mar - May

Interesting Fact: The Opera House roof has over 1 million tiles!
