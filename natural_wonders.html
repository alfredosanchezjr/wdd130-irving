Natural Wonders

Explore nature's most breathtaking creations.

Grand Canyon, USA

A massive canyon carved by the Colorado River.

Best Time: Mar - May, Sep - Nov

Fun Fact: Over 277 miles long!

Mount Everest, Nepal/China

The tallest mountain in the world.

Best Time: Apr - May

Fun Fact: It grows about 4mm each year!

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

The world's largest coral reef system.

Best Time: Jun - Oct

Fun Fact: Visible from outer space!

Northern Lights, Iceland/Norway

A natural light display in the Arctic sky.

Best Time: Sep - Apr

Fun Fact: Caused by solar particles!

Victoria Falls, Zambia/Zimbabwe

One of the largest waterfalls in the world.

Best Time: Feb - May

Fun Fact: Over 1,700 meters wide!
